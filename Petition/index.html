Thumbtack Petition for Change

Overview

I am leading a committee to change the way that Thumbtack practices business. Currently Thumbtack does not support the needs and wants of their true customer, the professional, and I am tried of paying for credits for customers that are not hiring. I am not alone and to prove that I am organizing a petition for Thumbtack professionals. After the petition reaches 500 professionals I will meet with thumbtack to lobby for change.

List of Demands

  • Refund credits to professionals if the customer does not hire someone after one week
  • Restructure credit amount to quote based on budget rather then category

Please feel free to contact me to add a demand to this list
